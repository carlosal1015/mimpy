SinglePhase¶

class mimpy.models.singlephase.SinglePhase[source]¶

The class relies on the Mesh and MFD libraries to solve time dependent slighly-compressible flow problems (heat equation).

\[\phi \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t} = -\nabla \cdot \left( \frac{\rho}{\mu} K (\nabla p)\right) + q\]
